About Me

I am a first-year DPhil student at Oxford University advised by Professor Edith Elkind. Before that I studied Computer Science and Mathematics at Harvard University where my undergraduate thesis was advised by Ariel Proccacia.

Research

My research interests include social choice and fair division, mechanism design, machine learning, and fairness and data privacy.
